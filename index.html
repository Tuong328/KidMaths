KidMaths

How To Use:

  • In a math question, when you want a range of numbers put "%%variableName: low, high%%"
  • Example: %%x: 9,10%%
  • To calculate your answer, in the second text box, simply add the formula for your problem.
  • For example, if your variables are "x" and "y" and you have an addition problem, put x+y in the second textbox.

    • Example Input:
  • What is the area of a rectangle of width: %%width: 3, 5%% and height: %%height: 6, 9%%
  • Sally has %%x: 1,5%% apples, she gets %%y: 2,3%% more, how many does she have now?

    • Example Output:
  • What is the area from width: 3 and height: 6
  • Sally has 4 apples, she gets 2 more, how many does she have now?
